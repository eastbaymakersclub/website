The shop

Tools for real projects.

The space is being built for practical making: prototyping, fabrication, electronics, repair, art experiments, and the weird half-formed ideas that turn into good evenings.

Prusa
3D printers

Multiple Prusa printers, including an XL 5-tool, for prototypes, brackets, repairs, and the occasional tiny miracle.

Laser cutter

Cut, engrave, test, repeat, and bring flat materials into crisp finished parts.

Electronics bench

Soldering, scopes, debugging, and enough bench space to stop balancing parts on notebooks.

ShopBot PRS5 Alpha
CNC mill

Large-format routing for furniture, fixtures, signs, molds, jigs, and sturdy ideas.
